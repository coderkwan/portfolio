A Courier company offering shipment services between South Africa and Eswatini.

The Problem

A newly established company needs to be know. Ship Smart Freight is a new kid in the shipment block. And they needed someone who will understand their vision for growth. Growth alongside simplicity.

Ship smart freight needed a simple, minimal website that will reveal, to the potential customers, who they are and what they do. The simple solution they were looking should not just reveal them but should also make them more visible on search engines.

The Solution

I had to go with a simple tech stack; Next js, Tailwind CSS and obviusly hosted on vercel.

Not only the stack, but the design as well had to as simple as possible. I implimented a very minimal and traditional layout. I did'nt do anything fancy; a courier service has to be trusted and look authentic and not playful. That's what i aimed for.
