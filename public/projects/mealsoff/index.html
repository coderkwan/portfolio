A website were you will find your next meal alongside recipes, tutorials and ingrediants.

The Problem

People love exploring new cuisines, but finding authentic recipes with accurate ingredient lists can be challenging. Many recipe websites either lack diversity, fail to provide clear instructions, or do not cater to users looking for meals from different cultures in one place. Additionally, users often struggle with ingredient substitutions, portion adjustments, and an intuitive way to discover new dishes based on their preferences. Without a well-structured platform, exploring international recipes can feel overwhelming.

The Solution

I developed a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate website that categorizes meals by culture, making it simple for users to explore diverse cuisines. Each recipe includes a detailed ingredient list, step-by-step instructions, and suggested alternatives for hard-to-find ingredients. I implemented a dynamic search and filtering system, allowing users to discover recipes based on cuisine type, ingredients, or dietary preferences. The site is fully responsive, ensuring seamless access across devices. This solution provides a convenient and engaging experience for food enthusiasts looking to expand their culinary horizons
