A website for a modelling agency.

The Problem

The organization needed a simple website to promote their business support services and modelling classes. They had no clear way to share information or let people sign up. This made it hard for them to grow and attract new clients.

Industry

  • Modeling, Business

Solutions

  • Web design
  • Web development

Country

  • Eswatini

Tech Stack

  • Next Js
  • Tailwind CSS
  • Vercel

The Solution

I designed a modern, easy-to-navigate website using Next.js and Tailwind CSS, then deployed it on Vercel. The site presents their services in a clear, structured way and includes simple forms so visitors can easily get in touch or register. This gave the organization a strong online presence and a better way to connect with potential clients.
