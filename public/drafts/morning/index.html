The greatest responsibility is taking care of oneself. I must see to it that God finds no fault in me. I must not degrade my body and reason to be like that of wild beasts.

I will aim for balance in all things. I will do good and avoid extremes. I will be concern only of my business, my will, what I can control.

I will let fate be fate. I will let the master dominate in all events. Who should I control the uncontrollable? Why should I fear the natural? Is the uncontrollable formidable? Why should I complain of yesterday?

Joyful I must be at all ways.
