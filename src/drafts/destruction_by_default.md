# Forced to be destructed

<sup>_07-07-2025_<sup>

It is increasingly getting hard to focus. Everything is now built with destruction as a feature. The car; now has TV, games, you can connect your phone and recieve calls. It is not solely for transportation but a bunch of other things as well, good enough to get you destructed.

My main focus is not cars, I am just making an example. What caught my attention was the internet, mostly the social platforms; Blogs, Scocial Media, Streaming platforms. In all this platforms it is impossible to focus on what you want. As you watch this video, 7 other videos are  pushed in your face. As you're reading a post,14 adverts and 3 recommended post are shoved down your throt. You can't be satisfied with what you're consuming, you also longing for that glitering algorithm-recommended clip on the sidebar.

It is clear that  most creators and manufactures careless about your time and satifaction. And the responsibility is left with you. You are the one to pave your way into a focused life. You are the armed guard of your precious time. All this click temptations has to be resisted by will power.

Because all platforms are desctruction packed by default, it is now a norm. And people are hardly aware of it.

And I now understand why folks like Cal Newport preach about digital minimalism; It is hard to resist the algorithm. It is very well designed not just to destruct you but to tempt you and keep you on the platform as long as possible.
